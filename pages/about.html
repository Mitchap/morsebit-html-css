MorseBit is a design lab focused on the development of aesthetically functional apparel. Developing apparel that is technically capable to adapat with the demands of the user's environment or present situation, fluctuating from a hot humid climate that favors more breathability and looser forms, to a cold, wet environment where water resistance and more form-fitting cuts are necessary, including functional straps and extra pockets for technical advantage is MorseBit's directive.
